### Challenge: Warmed Up
**Goal**
<br>
Converting a hexadecimal number into its corresponding decimal.
<br> 
**Answer**
<br>
• 0x - indicates that number is written in hexadecimal (base 16).
<br>
• 3D - (3*16)+(13*1)
<br>
• 0x3D = 61 (in decimal - base10)

**Flag**
```bash
picoCTF{61}
```
